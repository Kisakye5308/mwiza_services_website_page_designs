01.

Services Provided

Mwiza Services offers premium event planning, decor, and management services across Uganda. We are committed to delivering elite experiences tailored to our clients' unique visions.

Our services include, but are not limited to: luxury wedding planning, corporate gala management, bespoke floral design, and technical event production. All services are subject to availability and formal confirmation via a signed Service Level Agreement (SLA).

Note: Any modifications to the agreed scope of work must be documented in writing and may incur additional charges.

02.

Booking & Cancellations

To secure a date, a non-refundable commitment deposit of 30% of the total project value is required. Bookings are only considered "Confirmed" once the deposit has cleared in our accounts and the contract is signed by both parties.

  • Cancellations made more than 90 days prior to the event will forfeit the deposit only.
  • Cancellations made 30-89 days prior to the event will incur a fee of 60% of the total estimated cost.
  • Cancellations made less than 30 days prior to the event are non-refundable and require 100% payment.
03.

Payment Terms

The remaining balance (70%) must be settled at least 14 working days before the event date. Mwiza Services reserves the right to suspend work or cancel the booking if payments are not received according to the schedule.

We accept Bank Transfers, Mobile Money (Business Merchant), and major Credit Cards. Any transactional fees incurred through third-party payment processors are the responsibility of the client.
