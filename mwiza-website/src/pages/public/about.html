Our Story

Elevating African Excellence

Since 2018, Mwiza Services has been transforming ordinary events into extraordinary experiences across Uganda, setting the gold standard in luxury event management.

Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence

Mwiza Services was born from a simple vision: to bring world-class event experiences to Uganda while celebrating our rich African heritage. What started as a small team of passionate event enthusiasts has grown into the country's most trusted name in luxury event staffing.

Our name "Mwiza" means "beautiful" in Kinyarwanda, and beauty is at the heart of everything we do. From the elegant presentation of our ushers to the artistry of our cocktail mixologists, we believe that every detail matters.

Today, we're proud to have served over 1,200 events, from intimate private dinners to grand corporate galas and traditional Ugandan weddings that honor our cultural roots.
