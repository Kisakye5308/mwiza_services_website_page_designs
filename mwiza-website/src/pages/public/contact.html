Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services? expand_more
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for smaller events and 2-3 months for larger events like weddings or corporate galas. However, we do accommodate last-minute requests when possible.
Do you provide services outside of Kampala? expand_more
Yes! We serve clients throughout Uganda including Entebbe, Jinja, Mbarara, and other major cities. Transport and accommodation fees may apply for events outside Kampala.
What is your cancellation policy? expand_more
Cancellations made 14+ days before the event receive a full refund minus a 10% admin fee. Cancellations within 7-14 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 7 days before the event are non-refundable, though credits may be applied to future bookings.
Can I customize the service packages? expand_more
Absolutely! We understand every event is unique. Our team will work with you to create a customized package that fits your specific needs, preferences, and budget.
